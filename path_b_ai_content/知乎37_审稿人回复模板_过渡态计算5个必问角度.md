# 知乎37: 审稿人回复模板——过渡态计算5个必问角度（避坑系列#4）

---

> **系列定位**: DFT计算化学·避坑系列第4篇 | **字数**: ~2400字 | **品类**: 实操避坑·审稿攻防（致知计划高转化型）

---

上篇讲了NEB过渡态计算的5个致命陷阱，有人私信我："陷阱我记住了，但审稿人问的角度完全不一样——他问我为什么虚频只有-30 cm⁻¹，这正常吗？"

这就是审稿和踩坑的本质区别：**踩坑是自己算错，审稿是证明自己没算错。**

把上一篇文章的checklist做完只能保证你的结果是自洽的——但要说服审稿人，你需要的是"审稿攻防体系"。今天把过渡态计算审稿中最常见的5个必问角度一次性拆解，每个都附回复模板——不是教你糊弄审稿人，而是教你如何用数据和逻辑正面回应。

---

## 角度1: "你的过渡态真的收敛了吗？"

**审稿人原话变体**:
- "What force criterion did you use for TS optimization?"
- "The imaginary frequency seems rather small. Can you justify it?"
- "Did you verify the TS with a tighter convergence?"

**审稿人真正想问的**: 我不信你这个过渡态——你用力收敛的/用频率收敛的/用能量收敛的，到底哪个？

**你的回复三层结构**:

**第一层——列出收敛标准（精确到数字）**:
> We optimized all transition states using the dimer method (or CI-NEB followed by dimer) with the following convergence criteria: (1) maximum force on each atom < 0.02 eV/Å for TS optimization (tighter than the 0.05 eV/Å used for intermediate optimizations); (2) energy change between consecutive ionic steps < 1×10⁻⁵ eV; (3) the dimer curvature along the reaction coordinate converged to < 0.01 eV/Å². These criteria are consistent with those recommended by Henkelman et al. (J. Chem. Phys., 2000).

**第二层——虚频验证**:
> The optimized TS structure exhibits exactly one imaginary frequency of -XXX cm⁻¹, corresponding to the reaction coordinate. Visualization confirmed that the normal mode displaces the system from reactant toward product. No other imaginary modes were present (> -10 cm⁻¹ is considered negligible in periodic DFT).

**第三层——如果虚频特别小（< -50 cm⁻¹）怎么解释**:
> The relatively small imaginary frequency of -XX cm⁻¹ reflects a flat potential energy surface along this reaction coordinate, which is consistent with [物理理由：如氢原子扩散、弱吸附物种迁移等]. Similar magnitudes have been reported for [引1-2篇文献]. To further verify, we performed a single-point energy scan along the normal mode coordinate, confirming a well-defined maximum (Figure SX).

**常见错误**: 只说"converged to 0.02 eV/Å"但不提频率。审稿人默认"有力收敛+虚频验证"才叫过渡态收敛。

## 角度2: "你怎么证明这个过渡态连接的就是你声称的初态和末态？"

**审稿人原话变体**:
- "Did you perform IRC calculations to confirm the TS connects the proposed initial and final states?"
- "The TS geometry looks similar to the product. Is it really a TS for the forward reaction?"
- "How do you know this TS is for H₂O₂ dissociation rather than H₂O desorption?"

**这是审稿人最常问的角度，没有之一。** 原因：过渡态优化只找到势能面上的一个鞍点——它不知道自己在连接谁。

**回复模板**:

> **IRC Verification.** We performed intrinsic reaction coordinate (IRC) calculations from each TS in both forward and reverse directions using the method of Hratchian and Schlegel (J. Chem. Theory Comput., 2005) as implemented in VASP. Both directions converged smoothly to the expected reactant and product minima (Figure SX, 能量+结构快照), confirming the TS connects the claimed initial and final states.

关键细节（审稿人最爱追问的）:
- **Step size**: "A step size of 0.05 amu^(1/2)·Bohr was used; test calculations with 0.03 amu^(1/2)·Bohr showed identical paths."
- **收敛标准**: "IRC steps were considered converged when the energy gradient fell below 0.0005 eV/Å, matching the geometry optimization criterion for intermediates."
- **如果是多步反应**: "For the stepwise mechanism (TS1→IM→TS2), IRC from TS1 forward converged to IM, and IRC from TS2 reverse converged to the same IM, verifying pathway connectivity."

**最大加分项**: 附一张IRC能量图+插图标注初态/过渡态/末态的几何结构。一张好图顶500字回复。

## 角度3: "为什么你选这个泛函？换了泛函你的能垒还成立吗？"

**审稿人原话变体**:
- "Can you justify the choice of the PBE functional for this system?"
- "How sensitive is the barrier to the choice of exchange-correlation functional?"
- "Have you considered dispersion corrections / Hubbard U / hybrid functionals?"

**三层回复**:

**① 选定泛函的理由（不要只写"常用"，要写"适合"）**:
> PBE was chosen as it has been shown to accurately describe [你的体系特性] for [你的材料类型]. For example, [引用1: 相似体系用PBE验证过的文献], and [引用2: benchmark against hybrid functionals or experiments for your class of materials].

**② 特定校正的选择**:
> Dispersion corrections (DFT-D3 with Becke-Johnson damping) were included to account for van der Waals interactions, which are non-negligible for [吸附/层状/分子间]. A Hubbard U correction of U_eff = X eV was applied to [金属] 3d orbitals based on the linear response method (Cococcioni and de Gironcoli, PRB, 2005) / literature consensus.

**③ 泛函敏感性测试（最加分项）——如果计算资源允许**:
> To assess the sensitivity of our results to the choice of functional, we recalculated the key barrier (TS1, the rate-determining step) using [HSE06 / PBE+U with U varied by ±1 eV / RPBE / SCAN]. The barrier changed by only 0.XX eV (Table SX), confirming that our conclusions are not artifacts of the functional choice.

**如果换了泛函能垒确实变了怎么办？** 诚实说，但给出趋势理由:
> The barrier with HSE06 (X.XX eV) is 0.XX eV higher than with PBE (Y.YY eV), consistent with the known tendency of hybrid functionals to increase reaction barriers for [物理理由]. The trend across functionals (PBE < RPBE < HSE06) matches the benchmarks of [文献], and the qualitative conclusion—that TS1 is rate-determining—remains unchanged.

## 角度4: "你考虑熵效应了吗？温度对能垒的影响怎么处理的？"

**审稿人原话变体**:
- "The barriers reported are electronic energy barriers. Have you considered entropic contributions?"
- "The experimental conditions are at 300°C. How does temperature affect your computed barriers?"
- "Did you include ZPE corrections?"

**这是理论与实验对接的关键桥梁。** 审稿人问这个通常意味着他想要实验可比性。

**回复模板**:

> **Free Energy Barriers.** We calculated vibrational frequencies for all stationary points (reactant, product, and TS) within the harmonic approximation to obtain zero-point energy (ZPE) corrections and finite-temperature contributions (vibrational enthalpy and entropy). The free energy barrier at T = XXX K was evaluated as:
> ΔG‡ = ΔE_elec + ΔZPE + ΔH_vib(T) - TΔS_vib(T)
> 
> The resulting free energy barrier is X.XX eV (cf. electronic barrier Y.YY eV). The difference of 0.XX eV arises primarily from [ZPE difference / entropic stabilization of the TS / etc.].

**常见追问与应对**:
- "Harmonic approximation低估了软模的熵" → "For low-frequency modes (< 50 cm⁻¹), we checked that replacing them with a free-rotor/translational model changes the entropy by < 0.05 eV at 300°C (Table SX)."
- "溶剂效应呢？" → "For gas-phase heterogeneous catalysis, the entropic contribution from the surface is negligible compared to the molecular adsorbate. Solvent effects are outside the scope of this gas-phase DFT study."

**一条铁律**: 只要审稿人提到实验温度，你就必须给ΔG‡，不能只给ΔE‡。

## 角度5: "你的能垒和实验值能对上吗？对不上为什么？"

**审稿人原话变体**:
- "Can you compare your computed barrier with experimental values?"
- "The experimental apparent activation energy is X.XX eV, but your computed barrier is Y.YY eV. How do you reconcile this difference?"
- "If there is no experimental data, how can we trust the computed barrier?"

**这可能是所有审稿问题里最难的——因为运气不好的话，算出来的值和实验就是差很多。**

**三层应对**:

**① 如果有实验值且能对上（最理想）**:
> The computed free energy barrier of X.XX eV for the RDS agrees well with the experimental apparent activation energy of Y.YY eV reported by [文献] under similar conditions.

**② 如果有实验值但对不上（需要诚实+物理理由）**:
> The computed barrier (X.XX eV) is higher/lower than the experimental apparent activation energy (Y.YY eV). This discrepancy may arise from several factors: (a) the experimental value is an "apparent" activation energy that convolutes adsorption enthalpies with the intrinsic barrier—our microkinetic analysis (Section X) shows that after accounting for coverage effects, the effective barrier shifts to Z.ZZ eV, in better agreement; (b) the experimental catalyst differs from our model surface in [具体差异]; (c) surface defects or minority sites, not captured in our periodic slab model, may dominate the experimental kinetics.

**③ 如果没有实验值（最常见情况）**:
> While no direct experimental barrier measurement exists for this specific system, our computed barriers are internally consistent: (a) TS search and IRC verification were performed systematically for all elementary steps; (b) the resulting free energy diagram satisfies thermodynamic consistency (ΔG of the overall reaction equals the sum of ΔG of elementary steps); (c) the rate-determining step identified from our barriers agrees with the kinetic isotope effect / Tafel slope / selectivity trend reported in experimental studies of similar systems [引用].

**最关键的一句话（审稿人吃这套）**: "Our DFT-computed barriers serve as physically motivated initial estimates for microkinetic modeling—their absolute accuracy is secondary to the mechanistic insight they provide about the rate-determining step and selectivity trends."

## 一个真实教训

我一个师妹，算了一个很漂亮的H₂O₂解离过渡态，虚频-456 cm⁻¹，IRC完美连接。审稿人问"Can you rule out the possibility that this TS corresponds to H₂O desorption instead?"——她才发现自己算的初态里H₂O₂的O-O键已经拉长到了1.9 Å，相当于"还没开始算就已经解离了一半"。

审稿人的三个字"rule out"，本质上是在问：**你排除其他可能性的逻辑链条是什么？**

这也回到了本系列一再强调的核心：**DFT计算不做完不算完，说服了自己才能说服审稿人。**
